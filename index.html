Report Anonymously, Speak Up Safely

Reporting incidents of sexual and gender-based violence can be daunting, but you don’t have to face it alone. Our anonymous reporting feature allows you to share experiences in a secure and confidential environment.

Support Services

We understand that healing is a journey, and everyone’s needs are unique. That’s why we offer a comprehensive range of support services and resources tailored to survivors’ needs. From counseling and legal assistance to medical support and advocacy resources, we’re here to help you navigate your path to healing and recovery.

Counseling Unit

Our counseling unit is a specialized group offering professional guidance and support to individuals dealing with personal, emotional, or psychological challenges, aiming to enhance well-being and personal development.

Legal Aids Unit

Our legal aid unit provides free or low-cost legal assistance to individuals who cannot afford legal services, ensuring access to justice and representation for those facing legal challenges.

Medical Assistance's Unit

Our medical assistance unit provide healthcare support, to individuals in need, ensuring access to medical care and assistance for those facing health-related challenges
