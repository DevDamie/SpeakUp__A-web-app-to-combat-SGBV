About Us

We are a dedicated platform committed to fostering a safer and more supportive community. At speak up, we believe in the power of collective action to address and prevent sexual and gender-based violence. Our platform operates on the core values of confidentiality, empowerment, and advocacy. We understand the importance of privacy and ensure that all reports are handled with the utmost sensitivity and care. We believe in the strength of survivors and strive to provide them with the support and resources they need to heal and reclaim their lives.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a safe space for individuals to
anonymously report incidents of sexual and gender-based violence.
We aim to empower survivors, raise awareness, and
contribute to the creation of a world free such harm.

Why SpeakUp?

We understand the importance of anonymity in reporting
sensitive incidents.
Speakup was born out of the desire to offer a secure and
confidential platform, allowing survivors to share their
experiences without fear. We believe in the strength of
every voice and its potential to drive positive change.
